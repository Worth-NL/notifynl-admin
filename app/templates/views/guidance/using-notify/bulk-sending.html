Change the international text message limit

To change the international daily text message limit:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Select Change on the International text message limit row

When changing the limit, you should think about:

  • how many international messages your service needs to send each day
  • any costs to your service if there is fraud, such as Artificial Inflation of Traffic (AIT)

If you do not need to send international text messages, you can turn this off in Settings.

Help protect your service from AIT
You should take steps to protect your service from an AIT attack by:
  • tracking how many messages you send and set an alert in your system for anything that is not normal
  • stopping multiple messages being sent to the same number
Find out what you can do to help protect your service from AIT.
